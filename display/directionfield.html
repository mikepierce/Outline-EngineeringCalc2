Direction Fields

Examples from Stewart

  1. Consider the differential equation \(\dot y = x+y\).
    1. Sketch a direction field for this differential equation.
    2. Using your direction field, sketch the curve of the particular solution corresponding to initial condition \(y(0) = 1\).
  2. Consider the differential equation \(\dot y = x^2+y^2-1\).
    1. Sketch a direction field for this differential equation.
    2. Using your direction field, sketch the curve of each of the particular solutions that correspond to initial conditions \(y(0) = 0\), \(y(0) = 2\), and \(y(1) = -2\).
