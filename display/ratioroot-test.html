Ratio and Root Test for Series

Examples from Stewart

  1. Are these series absolutely convergent? \[ \sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^n\frac{n^3}{3^n} \qquad \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{n^n}{n!} \]
  2. Are these series absolutely convergent? \[ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \left(\frac{2n+3}{3n+2}\right)^n \qquad \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty \left(\frac{n}{n+1}\right)^n \]

Additional Exercises

  1. Are these series absolutely convergent? \[ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \left(\arctan(n)\right)^n \qquad \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{(2n)!}{(n!)^2} \]
  2. Show that regardless of the value of \(x\), the series \[\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{x^n}{n!} \] converges, and realize that this means \( \lim_{n\to\infty} x^n/n! = 0 \) for any number \(x\).
