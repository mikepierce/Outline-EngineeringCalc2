Power Series

Examples from Stewart

  1. For what values of \(x\) does this series converge? \[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(x-3)^n}{n}\]
  2. For what values of \(x\) does this series converge? \[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} n! x^n \]
  3. For what values of \(x\) does this series converge? \[\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{(2n)!} \]
  4. What is the radius of convergence and the interval of convergence of this series? \[\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-3)^n x^n}{\sqrt{n+1}} \]
  5. What is the radius of convergence and the interval of convergence of this series? \[\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{n (x+2)^n}{3^{n+1}} \]
