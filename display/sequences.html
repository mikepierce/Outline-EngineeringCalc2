Sequences

Examples from Stewart

  1. Decide if the following sequences converge or diverge, and if they converge calculate their limit. \[ \left\{(-1)^n\right\} \quad \left\{\frac{(-1)^n}{n}\right\} \quad \left\{\sin(n)\right\} \quad \left\{\frac{n!}{n^n}\right\} \]
  2. For which values of \(r\) does the sequence \(\{r^n\}_{n=1}^{\infty}\) converge?
  3. Write a convincing argument that the sequence \[\left\{\frac{n}{n^2+1}\right\}\] is decreasing.
