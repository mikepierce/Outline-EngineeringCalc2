Alternating Series

Examples from Stewart

  1. Verify, in detail, that this series converges \[\sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n+1}\frac{n^2}{n^3+1}\]
  2. Does this series converge or diverge? \[\sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n-1}\frac{3n}{4n-1}\]
  3. Does this series converge or diverge? \[\sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{\cos(n)}{n^2}\]
  4. For what values of \(p\) is this series convergent? \[\sum_{n=2}^\infty (-1)^{n-1}\frac{\big(\ln(n)\big)^p}{n}\]
  5. Determine whether each of these series is absolutely convergent, conditionally convergent, or divergent. \[ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{(-1)^{n}}{n^3} \qquad \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{(-1)^{n}}{\sqrt[3]{n}} \qquad \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n}\frac{n}{2n+1} \]
