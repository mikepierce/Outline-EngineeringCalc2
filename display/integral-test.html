Integral Test for Series

Examples from Stewart

  1. Are these series convergent or divergent? \[ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{n^2+1} \qquad \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{\ln(n)}{n} \]

Additional Exercises

  1. Why can't you apply the integral test to decide the convergence/divergence of these series? \[ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{\cos(\pi n)}{\sqrt{n}} \qquad \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{\cos^2(n)}{1+n^2} \]
  2. For which values of \(p\) will this series converge? \[\sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{\ln(n)}{n^p}\]
