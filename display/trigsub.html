Trigonometric Substitution

  1. (Spivak) Evaluate the following integrals. Only do as many as it takes you to feel confident that you can recognize the correct substitution. \[ \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1+x^2} \quad \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1-x^2} \quad \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \] \[ \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{1+x^2}} \quad \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{x^2-1}} \quad \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^2-1}} \] \[ \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{1-x^2}} \quad \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{1+x^2}} \quad \int x^3\sqrt{1-x^2}\,\mathrm{d}x \] \[ \int \sqrt{1-x^2}\,\mathrm{d}x \quad \int \sqrt{1+x^2}\,\mathrm{d}x \quad \int \sqrt{x^2-1}\,\mathrm{d}x \]
  2. Evaluate the following integrals. Solutions can be found in Stewart. \[ \int \frac{\sqrt{9-x^2}}{x^2} \,\mathrm{d}x \qquad \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^2\sqrt{x^2+4}} \] \[ \int \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+4}} \,\mathrm{d}x \qquad \int\limits_{0}^{3\sqrt{3}/2} \frac{x^3}{(4x^2+9)^{3/2}} \,\mathrm{d}x \] \[ \int \frac{x}{\sqrt{x-2x-x^2}} \,\mathrm{d}x \]
  3. Evaluate the following integrals. Solutions can be found in Paul's Notes. \[ \int \sqrt{1-7\omega^2} \,\mathrm{d}\omega \qquad \int\limits_{-7}^{-5} \frac{2\mathrm{d}y}{y^4\sqrt{y^2-25}} \] \[ \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{9x^2-36x+37}} \]
